State of the Map 2022

State of the Map is the annual event for all mappers and OpenStreetMap users. In 2022 the State of the Map conference will be a three day hybrid conference taking place in Florence, Italy and online. Please consider signing up for updates via our newsletter, join our telegram group chat and follow us on Twitter.

The conference will take place before the FOSS4G 2022 conference, which is also in Firenze on August 22 to 28, 2022. The local team is committed to providing participants with a safe conference environment in accordance with the latest safety and health guidelines set by authorities.
